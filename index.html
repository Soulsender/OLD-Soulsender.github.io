Hello, I'm

I'm a Canadian student studying computer science and cybersecurity. I'm a passionate Linux user, known for my custom desktops. I most commonly use Debian-based operating systems - for my daily desktop use, and in cloud-based SoHo servers of my own design.

What do I do?

A multiude of things.

I'm always learning new things, so I don't specilize in one area in particular. I hope to one day be an expert and have advanced knowledge of computers and cybersecurity.

Programming

I make programming projects such as automated bots, and applications. I've learned to use API documentation for things like Discord, as well as have done some rudimentary website development. I am also able to write bash scripts for automating shell activity. You may check out my projects over on GitHub! (linked below)

Linux and Servers

I love to see the inner workings of computers, and I use Linux operating systems on a daily basis. I am comfortable in the terminal, being able to edit firewall permissions, create VPN servers, remotely SSH into said servers, and manage desktop enviroments.

Hacking

Along with my ever-growing knowledge of Linux operating systems, I compete in Capture the Flag (CTFs) hacking competitions, where hackers compete to breach and steal a flag from a machine. CTFs can be divided into different types of hacks, such as web, cryptography, forensics, reverse engineering, and even more!
